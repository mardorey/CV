Resourcer to Business Manager

This role saw me running the recruitment function across various Countries and Continents for some of the world’s leading companies. My roles have led me to working successfully with individuals from Graduates and entry level candidates right through to Managing Directors, Engineering Managers and HR Directors/Business Partners.

I have led teams of recruiters, ranging from Trainee Consultants through to Senior Recruitment Consultants and have trained people within the sector in terms of the recruitment process, business development, time and database management.
I have worked with companies across the globe, having managed divisions in the UK, Europe, Africa and South East Asia, and have travelled across continents to develop new business and maintain existing relationships.
